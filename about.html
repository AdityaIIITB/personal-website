About Me

A little bit about my background and interests.

My Story

Hi there! I'm driven by two core passions: building impactful technology products and cultivating personal discipline. Professionally, my journey has taken me through software engineering and product management roles, including contributing to significant SaaS transformations during my time at VMware. Now, pursuing my MBA, I'm focused on amplifying that technical foundation with strategic business insights. But beyond the screen and the strategy sessions, I believe that peak performance starts with holistic well-being.

Discipline isn't just a concept for me; it's a practice woven into my daily life. I prioritize physical fitness and maintain a healthy diet, understanding that a strong body fuels a sharp mind. This commitment was put to the test when I decided to step into the boxing ring for an amateur bout. The intense training, the mental fortitude required, and the sheer physical challenge was an unforgettable lesson in pushing boundaries and discovering inner strength.

To complement the physical exertion and the mental demands of my tech career and studies, I rely heavily on meditation. It's my essential tool for staying grounded, sharpening focus, and navigating challenges with greater clarity. Whether it's tackling a complex problem, preparing for a demanding workout, or simply finding stillness in a busy day, this practice is fundamental to how I operate and strive to bring my best self to everything I do.
